## 2. üìä Midnight Daily Tracker (12am daily)

**What it does:**  
At midnight, your AI reviews the day ‚Äî what got done, what didn't, any important decisions ‚Äî and logs it. Creates an automatic daily record.

**Why it matters:**  
You'll never ask "wait, when did I decide that?" again. Every decision, blocker, completed task, and open question gets logged automatically. It's like having a chief of staff taking notes in every meeting ‚Äî except the meeting is your entire day.

**Benefits:**
- Instant recall of past decisions
- Track velocity over time (what's actually getting done?)
- Spot patterns (same blocker 3 days in a row? Fix it.)
- Onboard team members faster ("here's what happened last week")

### üìã COPY-PASTE PROMPT:

Save this to a daily log file (memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md). Keep it concise.

### üìã COPY-PASTE PROMPT:
