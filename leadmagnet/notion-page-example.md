## How It Works

### The Orchestrator Pattern

Most people use AI like a chatbot. You ask, it answers. That's one brain doing everything.

This system is different. The orchestrator is the brain, but it doesn't execute. It routes.

**Example:**

You say: *"Write a Twitter thread about AI agents."*

Orchestrator thinks: *"That's a writing task. But it needs research first."*

1. Spawns **Researcher**: "Find current AI agent trends, top discussions, use cases."
2. Waits for research output.
3. Spawns **Writer**: "Using this research, write a 10-tweet thread. Hook-first, operator tone."
4. Delivers final thread to you.

**Result:** Better output, faster execution, cleaner context.

Each agent is a specialist. They do one thing extremely well. The orchestrator coordinates them.
